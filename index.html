A digital footprint is the trail you leave behind on the internet. Everything from a comment on an instagram post to your name appearing on the news is permentant and can be found on the web so its best you follow these simple steps to make sure you havn't left a suspicious trail and prevent and future ones.

1. Constantly Google Yourself - The best way to check your online presence is googling your name and checking if there is any bad or otherwise unappealing information.

2. Monitor your social media accounts - Go back and look through your old posts to see if there might be anything that would make you look bad.

3. Think twice before you post anything - Anything you say or do on the internet is permanent, so before hitting post make sure you run a confirmation through your mind the the post is positive.

4. NEVER, and I mean NEVER give out personal inoformation - Staying anonymous is always preferable when online. Handing out your address, phone number, or social secuirity number can compromise any precaustion you've done so far.
